A little more About Us:

ADAPT Technology is a USA technology based engineering company that offers our customers a range of quality products and services to enhance and compliment their overall product strategies. The company was founded by a team of engineering professionals that have formulated industry leading best practices from our lessons learned at 3 former successful engineering service firms we owned and operated.

ADAPT Technology has a strong customer base including, the US Army, GM, Hyundai, Toyota, ZEISS Group, and various more fortune 500 companies in a wide variety of industries; including Automotive, Defense, Heavy Equipment & Consumer Products. As a result, we fully understand the requirements of design and manufacturing enabling us to take an extremely innovative, yet practical approach to problem solving.

SPECIFIC TO DEFENSE:
ADAPT Technology has worked on various government and private sector projects in the past. We are very proud to say we have completely parametrically 3D modeled multiple 6000+ part US Army vehicles. We feel our CAD / CAE / METROLOGY processes are the most efficient available and have set a new standard for future projects. We have experience with smaller projects as well, including armor protection, telecommunication devices, steering systems and the analysis/certification of K4, K8, K12 crash protection barriers.

 

SPECIFIC to AUTOMOTIVE & TRANSPORTATION:
Our staff has worked on body in white, underbody, floor systems, chassis systems, seating systems, safety systems, powertrain systems, interior systems and many other commodities consisting of concept design through production launch in the plant.

SPECIFIC to HEAVY TRUCK & EQUIPMENT:
We have engineered, designed and analytically tested high load/vibration grit blasters consisting of over 3000 parts, semi-truck sleeper cabins, seat systems, frame stabilizers, clamping devices and more consisting of concept through production launch in the plant.

The ADAPT Michigan facility is positioned close to many of our major customer technical centers and we can accept large customer Trucks and Equipment directly into our work shop. Our technical staff along with CAD, CAE and METROLOGY equipment is mobile allowing us to set-up a technical program team at client sites or wherever is needed to expedite a project.
